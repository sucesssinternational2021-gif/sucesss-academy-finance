Important Warning

Resetting term data is a critical operation that cannot be easily undone. This will archive current term data and prepare the system for a new academic term.

Before proceeding, ensure you have:

  • Backed up all important data
  • Generated final reports for the current term
  • Collected all outstanding fees
  • Informed all staff about the term transition
